Animated Income Visualisations

I was commissioned (by the former CEO of Twitch(!)) to create animated visualisations of US income over time. The data preparation and prototyping was done ith Python, Pandas, and pyplot. The polished visualisations were created with html, and D3.js.

AISafety.world and AIGov.world

I built interactive maps of major organisations, funders, and researchers in the AI Safety and AI governance fields. These use D3.js to assemble a scatter plot of all the organisations using up to date data from a google sheet. It also took a lot of mucking around with the HTML and CSS to get the look right. These maps showcase dragging, panning, tooltips, CSS styling, HTML, design, and hover-to-expand.
